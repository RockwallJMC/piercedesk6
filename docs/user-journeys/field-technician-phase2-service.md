# Field Technician User Journey - Phase 2 Service Desk

## Role Overview

**Primary Responsibilities:**
- Execute service calls and maintenance tasks efficiently and safely
- Diagnose and resolve technical issues with installed security systems
- Document service activities and maintain accurate work records
- Communicate effectively with clients and maintain professional relationships
- Ensure compliance with safety protocols and quality standards
- Contribute to continuous improvement through feedback and knowledge sharing

**Key Performance Indicators:**
- First-call resolution rate and service quality scores
- Daily productivity and billable hours efficiency
- Client satisfaction ratings and professional conduct
- Safety compliance and incident-free performance
- Technical skill development and certification maintenance

## Daily Workflow Scenarios

### Morning Routine (6:30 AM - 7:30 AM)

**Scenario**: Daily preparation and route planning

1. **Mobile App Check-In** (Mobile Service App)
   - Review daily service assignments and priorities
   - Check route optimization and travel time estimates
   - Verify client contact information and site access details
   - Download technical documentation and service history

2. **Vehicle and Equipment Preparation**
   - Conduct pre-trip vehicle inspection and safety check
   - Verify tool inventory and service parts availability
   - Load specialized equipment for scheduled service calls
   - Check mobile device connectivity and app functionality

3. **Technical Preparation**
   - Review service ticket details and client requirements
   - Study system documentation and previous service history
   - Identify potential challenges and required resources
   - Plan service approach and time allocation

**Expected Outcome**: Fully prepared for efficient and professional service delivery

### Service Call Execution (8:00 AM - 4:00 PM)

**Scenario**: Professional service delivery with real-time documentation

1. **Client Arrival and Check-In**
   - Arrive at client site and announce presence professionally
   - Verify service requirements and access permissions
   - Conduct safety assessment and establish work area
   - Update service ticket status to "On Site" via mobile app

2. **Service Execution and Documentation**
   - Diagnose issues using systematic troubleshooting approach
   - Execute repairs or maintenance according to procedures
   - Document work performed with photos and detailed notes
   - Test system functionality and verify proper operation

3. **Client Communication and Completion**
   - Explain work performed and system status to client
   - Provide maintenance recommendations and preventive advice
   - Obtain client signature for work completion
   - Update service ticket with completion details and photos

4. **Real-Time Updates** (Throughout Day)
   - Update ticket status at each service call milestone
   - Upload photos and documentation immediately after completion
   - Communicate delays or issues to dispatch in real-time
   - Request additional resources or technical support as needed

**Expected Outcome**: High-quality service delivery with complete documentation

### End of Day Routine (4:30 PM - 5:30 PM)

**Scenario**: Daily wrap-up and preparation for next day

1. **Service Documentation Completion**
   - Complete any remaining service ticket documentation
   - Upload final photos and technical notes
   - Submit time entries and expense reports
   - Update inventory usage and parts consumption

2. **Vehicle and Equipment Maintenance**
   - Conduct post-trip vehicle inspection
   - Secure tools and equipment for overnight storage
   - Restock commonly used parts and supplies
   - Report any equipment issues or maintenance needs

3. **Next Day Preparation**
   - Review tomorrow's service assignments
   - Identify any special tools or parts needed
   - Plan optimal route and schedule logistics
   - Download technical documentation for upcoming calls

**Expected Outcome**: Complete daily documentation with preparation for next day success

2. **Knowledge Sharing and Feedback**

## Pain Points and Solutions

### Pain Point 1: Mobile Connectivity Issues
**Challenge**: Poor cellular coverage in basements and server rooms
**Solution**: Offline-capable mobile app with automatic sync
**Feature**: Mobile app with offline documentation and sync capabilities

### Pain Point 2: Technical Documentation Access
**Challenge**: Difficulty accessing current technical manuals and guides
**Solution**: Integrated knowledge base with offline access
**Feature**: Technical knowledge base with downloadable documentation

### Pain Point 3: Parts Availability Uncertainty
**Challenge**: Uncertainty about parts availability for service calls
**Solution**: Real-time inventory visibility and parts reservation
**Feature**: Mobile inventory access with parts availability and reservation

### Pain Point 4: Complex Troubleshooting Support
**Challenge**: Limited access to senior technical support during service calls
**Solution**: AI-powered troubleshooting assistant with escalation options
**Feature**: AI Tech Assist with real-time troubleshooting guidance

### Pain Point 5: Administrative Burden
**Challenge**: Time-consuming paperwork and documentation requirements
**Solution**: Streamlined mobile documentation with voice-to-text
**Feature**: Mobile app with voice notes, photo capture, and automated forms
