# Service Manager User Journey - Phase 2 Service Desk

## Role Overview

**Primary Responsibilities:**
- Oversee service operations and SLA compliance across client portfolio
- Manage service team performance and resource allocation
- Monitor service profitability and operational efficiency
- Ensure client satisfaction and service quality standards
- Coordinate warranty claims and manufacturer relationships
- Drive continuous improvement in service delivery processes

**Key Performance Indicators:**
- SLA compliance rate and response time performance
- Service team utilization and productivity metrics
- Client satisfaction scores and retention rates
- Service profitability and margin optimization
- First-call resolution rate and repeat service minimization

## Daily Workflow Scenarios

### Morning Routine (7:00 AM - 8:00 AM)

**Scenario**: Daily service operations review and priority setting

1. **Service Dashboard Review** (`/desks/service`)
   - Review overnight service requests and emergency calls
   - Check SLA compliance status across all active tickets
   - Monitor service team availability and current assignments
   - Identify high-priority issues requiring immediate attention

2. **SLA Monitoring** (`/desks/service/sla`)
   - Review tickets approaching SLA breach thresholds
   - Analyze response time performance by service type
   - Check escalation queues and management notifications
   - Plan resource reallocation to meet SLA commitments

3. **Team Performance Review** (`/desks/service/tickets`)
   - Review technician productivity and ticket resolution rates
   - Check for overloaded technicians requiring support
   - Monitor skill-based routing effectiveness
   - Identify training needs and performance issues

**Expected Outcome**: Clear understanding of service operations status and daily priorities

### Mid-Morning: Service Coordination (8:30 AM - 10:00 AM)

**Scenario**: Active service management and resource optimization

1. **Ticket Triage and Assignment**
   - Review new service requests and emergency calls
   - Assign tickets based on technician skills and availability
   - Prioritize tickets based on SLA requirements and client impact
   - Coordinate with dispatch for optimal routing and scheduling

2. **Client Relationship Management**
   - Contact clients with service updates and status reports
   - Address client concerns and service quality issues
   - Coordinate scheduled maintenance and preventive service
   - Manage client expectations for complex or extended repairs

3. **Vendor and Warranty Coordination** (`/desks/service/warranty`)
   - Process warranty claims for equipment under coverage
   - Coordinate with manufacturers for technical support
   - Manage vendor relationships and service agreements
   - Track warranty expiration dates and renewal opportunities

**Expected Outcome**: Optimized service delivery with proactive client communication

### Afternoon: Performance Analysis (1:00 PM - 3:00 PM)

**Scenario**: Service performance analysis and process improvement

1. **Performance Metrics Analysis** (`/desks/service/reports`)
   - Analyze service team productivity and efficiency metrics
   - Review first-call resolution rates and repeat service patterns
   - Monitor service profitability by client and service type
   - Identify trends in service requests and equipment failures

2. **Quality Assurance Review**
   - Review completed service tickets for quality compliance
   - Analyze client feedback and satisfaction scores
   - Identify service quality issues and improvement opportunities
   - Plan quality improvement initiatives and training programs

3. **Recurring Service Management** (`/desks/service/recurring`)
   - Review recurring maintenance agreements and schedules
   - Monitor preventive maintenance completion rates
   - Analyze recurring service profitability and efficiency
   - Plan contract renewals and service agreement updates

**Expected Outcome**: Data-driven insights for service optimization and quality improvement

### End of Day: Planning and Reporting (4:00 PM - 5:30 PM)

**Scenario**: Daily wrap-up and strategic planning

1. **Daily Performance Reporting**
   - Compile daily service metrics and performance summaries
   - Update executive dashboard with key performance indicators
   - Document service challenges and resolution strategies
   - Prepare client status reports and communications

2. **Next Day Planning**
   - Review tomorrow's scheduled service appointments
   - Plan resource allocation and technician assignments
   - Identify potential service challenges and mitigation strategies
   - Schedule client meetings and vendor coordination calls

3. **Strategic Service Planning**
   - Review service contract renewals and expansion opportunities
   - Plan service team development and training initiatives
   - Analyze market trends and competitive service offerings
   - Develop service improvement and efficiency initiatives

**Expected Outcome**: Clear service strategy and operational plan for continued success

## Pain Points and Solutions

### Pain Point 1: SLA Management Complexity
**Challenge**: Difficulty tracking and managing multiple SLA requirements across clients
**Solution**: Automated SLA monitoring with proactive alerts and escalation
**Feature**: `/desks/service/sla` with real-time compliance tracking and notifications

### Pain Point 2: Service Profitability Visibility
**Challenge**: Limited visibility into service profitability by client and service type
**Solution**: Real-time service cost tracking and margin analysis
**Feature**: `/desks/service/reports` with profitability analytics and trending

### Pain Point 3: Technician Skill Matching
**Challenge**: Inefficient assignment of service tickets to appropriate technicians
**Solution**: Skill-based routing with automated assignment recommendations
**Feature**: `/desks/service/tickets` with intelligent assignment algorithms

### Pain Point 4: Warranty Claim Processing
**Challenge**: Manual warranty claim processing leading to delays and lost revenue
**Solution**: Automated warranty tracking and claim submission workflows
**Feature**: `/desks/service/warranty` with manufacturer integration and automation

### Pain Point 5: Client Communication Consistency
**Challenge**: Inconsistent client communication and status updates
**Solution**: Automated client notifications with customizable communication templates
**Feature**: Update Post Walls with client-facing communication automation
